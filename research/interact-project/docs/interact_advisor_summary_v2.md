# Interact Advisor Requirements (Concise)

## Who Must Serve

**MANDATORY:** One school faculty member as primary advisor

**RECOMMENDED:** 2-3 Rotarians as co-advisors (shared workload)

---

## Mandatory Requirements

**All Advisors Must:**
1. Complete Rotary youth protection training
2. Pass background screening
3. Attend all club meetings (~2x monthly)
4. Annual recertification

---

## Key Responsibilities

**Faculty Advisor:**
- Ensure school policy compliance
- Primary student contact during school hours

**All Advisors:**
- Guide (don't control) student-led meetings
- Support 2 annual service projects (1 community + 1 international)
- Coordinate logistics and resources
- Maintain records and fund oversight
- Connect to Rotary network and training opportunities

**Core Principle:** Students lead, advisors facilitate

---

## Time Commitment

- **Faculty:** 2-4 hours/month (mostly during school)
- **Rotarians:** 4-6 hours/month (meetings + project support)
