# Interact Club Membership Requirements

## Eligibility

**Age:** 12-18 years old OR until graduation, whichever is later

**Character:** Good character and leadership potential required

---

## Admission Process

**Method determined by:**
- Club itself (students decide)
- Approved by sponsor club (Rotary Georgetown)
- Approved by school (if school-based club)

**No specific process mandated** - club creates its own in bylaws

---

## Club Size

**No minimum or maximum** specified in constitution

Typical Interact clubs: 15-30 members, but can be smaller or larger

---

## Membership Ends Automatically

1. When student turns 18 OR graduates (whichever is later)
2. When club terminates
3. If attendance requirements not met (unless excused by board)

---

## Termination for Cause

Requires 2/3 vote of all members in good standing

---

## Key Point for Stonyhurst

**Students control membership decisions** (with sponsor/school approval)

This includes:
- How new members join
- Attendance requirements
- Removal for cause

Faculty advisor and Rotarians approve, but don't control admissions.
