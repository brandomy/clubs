## Company Profile

Founded in 2003, Brother Medical Factory is a top 5 fastest growing durable medical equipment manufacturer in the industry. They carry a complete line of durable medical equipment including mobility products, wheelchairs, commode chairs, shower chairs, walking sticks, lift chairs, crutches, mobility scooters, all kinds of rehabilitation products and personal home care products with CE, FDA, FSC certifications.
