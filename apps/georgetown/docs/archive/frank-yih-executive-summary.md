**For: Chairman PSR Frank Yih** | **Date:** December 2025

## The Problem
Service clubs in Asia cannot afford commercial platforms ($294-997/year) and are **blocked in China**. Program chairs manage 52 speakers per year through email chaos—**zero platforms offer speaker workflow tools**. Mobile apps are broken or missing.

## The Solution (FREE Forever)
**Service Club Manager** — Mobile platform for speaker scheduling, member directory, attendance, events, and service projects. Works in China (tested), offline-capable, multilingual (EN/中文/RU). Open-source, self-hosted.

**HuaQiao Bridge** (bridge.HuaQiao.asia) — Public directory connecting clubs globally for service project partnerships. **Strategic benefit to HQF:** Visibility into all club projects worldwide—easy to identify and cherry-pick projects HQF wants to support.
